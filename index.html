About Us.

Who we are:

We are just a bunch of bartenders who love this city and want to share it with all travellers and newbies who have a passion for beer and an even greater passion for fun! We even love it so much we're willing to spend our days off showing people Galway's coolest spots, so don't be shy, book a tour today!

What we do:

We are beer and whiskey afficionados who know just about all there is to know about three things: Beer, Whiskey and Galway city! We love Galway and take pride in our work as bartenders and tour guides and we want to share all our fantastic bars with you! Nothing new gets past us and we're constantly looking for new ways to improve what we do and make our tours the best they can possibly be. We also love making new friends and learning new things New to the city? Let us help you get nestled in with our famous weekly social meet-ups and language exchanges! Want something more outdoorsy/healthy? Join us for our weekly beer run!

Tours and Meet-Ups.

Tours

If you want to learn more about Irish spirits, draughts and the amazing Irish craft beer scene then these tours are for you! We'll take you to some of Galway's best kept secrets, where you'll drink the finest Irish whiskey and sip some of the tasiest beer both side of the atlantic all the while soaking up the atmosphere of the Irish pub scene and we'll even throw in some local tales to satisfy the curiousity of all you history buffs out there!

Meet Ups

Making friends in a new city is tough but it doesn't need to be! Let us help you get settled in and show you the best spots and people this city has to offer. Our meet-ups are free but donations are welcome. Locations change every week so you can get to know the city and meet new people at a nice relaxed pace. We also offer language exchanges for all you eternal students and we have a weekly beer run specifically for all you fitness fanatics! What are you waiting for? Get in touch today!
