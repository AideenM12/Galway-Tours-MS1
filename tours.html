Tours

Tour 1

  • Garavan's
  • Tigh Neachtains
  • Bierhaus

The tour begins in Garavan's where you'll sample one of their renowned whiskey platters and a crash course in Galway history. Next you'll soak up the cosy ambience of one of Galway's most traditional pubs and taste the creamiest Guinness in the city at Tigh Neactains and hopefully hear some famous trad music. Next off to the Bierhaus for best selection of local and internation craft beers and some top-notch cocktails!

Price:€20

Tour 2

  • Garavans
  • Freeneys
  • Salthouse
  • The Universal
  • Bierhaus

Tour 2 offers all the great stuff of Tour 1 with some special gems thrown in for good measure! First up Garavans for some great whiskey, followed by one of local's most popular haunts, Freeneys! Next enjoy some local Galway craft beer at Galway Bay Brewery's own pub, the Salthouse.

All that lovely drinking needs some tasty food so we'll stop for some delicious food and a cocktail at the Universal. The tour finishes in the Bierhaus where you can have some tasty cocktails or try rare treats from their draught beer selection.

Price:€40

Tour 3

Day 1
  • Garavans
  • Tigh Neactains
  • Bierhaus
  • The Universal
Day 2
  • Micil Distillery Tour
  • Oslo Bar and Microbrewery

Tour 3 offers something truly special. Day 1 offers all of the best bits of tours one and two but it's what happens on day 2 that makes it truly unique. On day 2 we take you out to the Micil Poitin Distillery.

Poitin is a spirit native only to Ireland, in particular Galway. Micil distill their poitin according to a family method that spans back 7 generations, predating the famine. Here you'll learn everything there is to know about the history and making of poitin whilst also tasting Micil's premium gin which along with the poitin is made on site. After the tour wash down some delicious chicken wings with some locally produced Galway Bay Brewery beer at Galway Bay Brewery's own Oslo Bar.

Price:€60
