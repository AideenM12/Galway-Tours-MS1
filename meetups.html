Meet-Ups and Language Exchange

Meet-ups

Moving to a new place can be very daunting but we want to make it as easy as possible! We meet twice a week in changing locations so you can get used to the city while you make new friends! You can send us a message or simply show up at the specified location. Tell us a bit about yourself and we'll do our best to match you up with similar minded folk! Our meet-ups are completely free but if you're feeling generous donations are always welcome!

This week's Meet-ups:

Our Wednesday Meet-Up starts a 7pm in The Blue Note pub on William St

Our Sunday Meet-Up begins at 6.30pm in The Dew-Drop-Inn on Bridge St

Come on down and get social!

Language Exchange

Nothing builds confidence when learning a new language more than speaking and what makes speaking easier?Beer! We meet every Thursday to practice languages together. Whether you're just starting out or you've got an important exam looming we've got the perfect study buddy for you! Membership is €15 a month but first exchange is free!

Where? Dov'é? Woher? Dónde? Cá háit?

This week's exchange begins at 7.30pm in The Bierhaus on Henry St

Who says studying can't be fun?

Beer Run!

We're all about health and fitness here at Galway Beer and Whiskey Tours! We run every Monday in tandem with Galway Bay Brewery Running Club. The run begins at the Oslo bar in Salthill, we run along the beautiful Salthill promenade for roughly about 5 km and afterwards we chow down on some wings and guzzle some much needed beers! During the summer we run every Sunday afternoon starting at the Salthouse on Raven's Terrace. New members always welcome and visitors are welcome to join during your stay.

Getting healthy is easier than you think!
